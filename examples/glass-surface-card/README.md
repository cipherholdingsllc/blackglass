# Glass Surface Card

## Purpose
Functional material tiering for hierarchy, focus, and boundary.

## States
- whisper
- present
- prominent

## Invariant
Glass must clarify state or depth; it never exists as decoration.

## Accessibility
Meaning survives through labels, borders, and contrast when blur or motion is reduced.

## Do-not-use-when (Avoid)
Do not apply glass to every repeated row.
