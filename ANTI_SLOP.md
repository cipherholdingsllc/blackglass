# Anti-Slop

Blackglass is built to reject generic AI interface output.

## Rejections

- generic AI gradients
- fake dashboards
- meaningless glass
- arbitrary neon
- random charts
- fake live telemetry
- one-off styling
- inaccessible motion
- noisy card grids
- copy that sounds inflated
- visual effects without a job

## Quality Gate

A Blackglass surface must answer:

- What is the job of this surface?
- What state is active?
- What should the viewer trust?
- What is next?
- What can be ignored?

If those answers are unclear, polish is premature.
