# Accessibility

Premium does not excuse unclear interfaces.

## Rules

- Preserve readable contrast on glass surfaces.
- Do not rely on color alone.
- Keep focus states visible.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences.
- Use semantic HTML where possible.
- Keep interactive controls reachable by keyboard.
- Avoid fake disabled states that still look active.
- Make status labels text-readable.

## Motion

All motion should be short and state-driven. Reduced-motion users should receive stable surfaces without losing meaning.

## Glass

Text on glass needs strong contrast and enough backing opacity. If a glass layer makes text harder to read, the layer is wrong.
